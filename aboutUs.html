About Us

The Dream Team

We are Americans
We are North Carolinians
We are aspiring web developers
but most importantly, we are concerned...

There's no need to beat around the bush. Covid-19 has been one of the most challenging trials our nation has faced in our time. It infects indescrimitnately, without mercy, and continues to expand it's reach and firm it's grip on our health as we try to balance social norms with social distancing.

Sadly, it's impact is felt even harder on our latino community. Over 33% of confirmed cases in the U.S. were of Hispanic or Latino descent, yet information and resources to help this community is scarce. It's our mission to pull in information from trusted, reliable sources to show you the current totals, updated daily, along with helpful tips, links and resources in an easy-to-use website to connect you to people who can help.

We are stronger together!
